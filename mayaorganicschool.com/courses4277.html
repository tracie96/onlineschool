DETAILED COURSE OUTLINE


Module 1.

INGREDIENTS AND EQUIPMENTS.

  • In this module you will discover gorgeous natural ingredients used in your products and Good Manufacturing Practice. You will learn more on:
  • Types of ingredients.(Ingredients names, converting measurement and calculating percentages, Preservative and antioxidants including natural preservative, emulsification and natural emulsifier.)
  • Equipment for formulating.
  • Suppliers list-where to buy your ingredients, packaging and materials.
  • Health and safety practises during production.
  • Good manufacturing practice work book.
  • Formulation skills(Understand how a formulation is created and structured.
  • Learn how to formulate a product from the scratch without following a receipe.
  • Study how to record your formulations in weight and percentages.

Module 2.

ESSENTIAL OILS AND CARRIER OILS.

  • In this module you will be taught the different types of essential oils and their shelf life. You will also learn more on:
  • Different kind of essential oils and carrier oils
  • skin benefits of essential oils, Carrier oils for skincare featuring 19 carrier oils, their important benefits and skin types.
  • Therapeutic properties.
  • How to dilute essential oil.
  • Essential oils dermal limit.

Module 3.

AFRICA BLACK SOAP.

  • This is commonly used in Africa and most Africans finds it difficult to make it from the scratch so they buy the solidify ones from the market and use it for soap business.
  • In this module we will be teaching you how to make Africa black soap from the scratch using luxurious oils and addictive for high performance result to give a beautiful flawless glowing complexion.
  • This is a new innovative method from Maya organic skincare school. You will also learn more on:
  • Black soap detox.
  • Black soap gel.
  • Baby black soap.
  • Lavender brightening black soap.

Module 4.

BAR SOAPS.

  • We love to teach you how to make beautiful unique bar soaps that contain skin lightening benefits without harsh chemicals, your customers will love this bar soaps. You will learn more on:
  • Coconut milk bar soap,this soap will leave your skin spotless and beautiful.
  • Turmeric bar soap for skin brightening.

Module 5.

DELICIOUS BODY BUTTERS AND LOTION BARS.

  • Our body butter in this module smells divine and it’s hydrating and moisturizing leaving the skin soft and smooth and can also help to repair damage bleached skin with stretch marks. You will learn more about:
  • Shea –coconut body butter.
  • Chamomile body butter for babies.
  • Peppermint foot butter.
  • Rosehip body butter for anti- aging.
  • Baby bump body butter.

Module 6.

OILS AND SERUM.

  • Oils and serums give the skin a youthful glow and appeal. It can also be used for body massage to reduce stress and body ache.
  • Discover the best oils to use ,how to make infused oil, moisturizing body and massage oils, cleansing oils and facial oils and serums. In this module you will learn more on:
  • Luxurious Facial serum
  • Eczema oil treatment.
  • Ylangylang body oil
  • Ginseng massage oil.
  • Carrot oil.
  • Avocado oil.

Module 7.

FACIAL MASK AND SCRUBS.

  • Facials mask and scrub are in serious demand in the skincare industry, most consumers are in search of preservative free masks and scrubs.
  • In this modules we are going to be showing you how to formulate with different kinds of clay and exfoliating ingredients benefits to these ingredients to the skin. We will cover sugar and salt –based scrubs, whipped butter-style scrubs, facial exfoliating lotions and a range of face masks. You will learn more on:
  • Emulsifying face scrub
  • Sensual exfoliating scrub for dark lip.
  • Mask powders with Natural herbs
  • Simple sugar scrub infused with herbs.
  • Body lightening polish for dark knuckles,elbows and thighs.
  • Lemon grass exfoliating lotion.

Module 8.

BODY FIRMING TREATMENT.

  • This course is new and it is designed specially for you. In this module you will be learning how to formulate product that can improve skin elasticity and blood circulation to avoid sagging. All ingredients used are 100% natural. You will learn more on:
  • Breast firming oil.
  • Bum bum enlargement oil.

Module 9.

FACIAL TONERS,SPRITZERS AND CLEANSERS.

  • In this modules we are using water soluble ingredients such aloevera, witch hazel, hydrosols and glycerine. You will learn more on:
  • Facial toners for normal/oily skin.
  • Rosemary and lavender toner
  • Spot treatment gel
  • Soothing chamomile eye gel
  • Witch hazel facial cleanser and more.

Module 10.

LOTIONS AND CREAMS.

  • Lotions are creams are fun to make and they come in different consistency –cream is thcker, lotion is lighter. In this module we are teaching you how to formulate lotion from the scratch using herbs, hydrosols, cosmeceuticals and essential oils.
  • We also provide a trouble shooting guide helping you fix any issues that might arise (such as creams separating) and a guide to adapting cream and lotion recipes. You will learn more on:
  • Face moisturizing lotion with rosehip oil.
  • Grapefruit body lotion.
  • Regenerating face cream with moringa oil.
  • Coconut body lotion
  • Lavender baby lotion and more.

Module 11.

BALMS.

  • Balms are nourishing, protective and moisturizing, simple to make and lovely to use on both hair and skin.
  • Discover the benefits of balms, different waxes you can use and how to vary the texture, you will also get our trouble shooting guide to help you fix common problem. You will learn more on:
  • Lemon grass body balm
  • Chamomile body balm for babies
  • Lip balm.
  • Peppermint foot balm
  • Geranium invigorating body balm and more.

Module 12.

BEAUTY BUSINESS SKILLS.

  • No matter how good your products if you don’t have the necessary skills to sell it to the consumers it will be of no benefit to you. In this module we are going to be teaching you how to find your target audience and promote your skincare brand to them.
  • We will teach you the importance of branding and marketing and provide you with materials to get started.
  • You will learn how to create a brand for your skincare range –including a brand name and your unique selling point.
  • You will also learn more about the global organic beauty market
  • Good Manufacturing practise (GMP).
  • NAFDAC registration process in Nigeria.
  • Product labelling ,Branding and packaging.
