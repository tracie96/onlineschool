DETAILED COURSE OUTLINE


Module 1.

INTRODUCTION TO HAIR CARE SCIENCE.

  • In the first module you will be learning more on:
  • Hair chemistry (Follicles and Growth cycle).
  • Components of the hair and scalp,Hair stand penetration.
  • Different types of natural hair, factors affecting hair growth and ageing of the hair.

Module 2.

HAIR CARE INGREDIENTS.

  • Ingredients are very key in creating unique formulations for hair care formulation the ingredients used are quite different from organic skincare formulation so in this course you will be learning more about ingredients used in formulating natural hair care products. You will also be learning how to calculate percentages in formulation.
  • Ingredients used in this course are plant –based,no animal products are used ,and no animal testing is done. All ingredients used are:
  • Paraben free
  • Sulphate free shampoos
  • No PEG’S
  • No Argtificial Fragrance
  • No Animal products.

Module 3.

HAIR SHAMPOOS.

  • This shampoo is great for removing all the dirts and grease from your hair. We will be learning:
  • Lavender deep cleansing shampoo.
  • Herbal hair shampoo
  • Conditioning shampoo

Module 4.

CONDITIONING TREATMENT.

  • Conditoners are very good on the hair,it restore the hair’s beauty and give the hair suppleness,softness and add gloss to the hair in this module we are going to be teaching 3 types of conditioners:
  • Organic leave in conditioner.
  • Creamy Condtioner with panthenol.
  • Cleansing conditioner or wash off conditioner.

Module 5.

HAIR OIL.

  • Hair oil are massage into the hair and left for a long period of time before washing out, in this module we are going to be showing you how to formulate with oils that can penentrate hair strand—this includes:
  • Basic strengthening hair oil
  • Hot oil treatment
  • Moringa wonder hair oil
  • Black castor oil anti-dandruff oil treatment.

Module 6.

HAIR BUTTER.

  • Hair butter provides weight to the hair while still conditioning the hair, it appeal to customers with dry, curl or coily hair types most especially 4c hair type which is common in( Africa).Hair butter de –tangling and conditions the hair. In this module we are going to be covering:
  • Detangling hair butter.
  • Hair twist butter
  • Sheabutter hair treatment.
  • Kiddies softening hair balm.

Module 7.

STYLING GEL.

  • Organic styling gels hold the hair in shape without using synthetic polymer in the module we are going to be teaching you how to formulate different types of hair gels:
  • Flaxseed gel.
  • Aloe vera gel.
  • Hair tonic.
  • Hair pudding.

Module 8.

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT/COSMETICCOMPLIANCE.

  • In this module we are going to be teaching you how to implement :
  • Good Manufacturing practise (GMP).
  • Product labelling , branding and packaging.
  • NAFDAC registration process in Nigeria.
  • How to work with your customers to help understand their exact hair care demands.
